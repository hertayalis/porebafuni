They say curiousity killed the cat, but the truth is that when your curious cat doesn’t have enough stimulation, they’ll probably kill your houseplants and ruin your furniture trying to get a little excitement out of life. You can supply your feline friend with cardboard scratchers and catnip-infused swatting toys, but one of the best ways to keep your cat stimulated on multiple levels (no pun intended) is by getting them a cat tower. The best cat towers combine spaces for climbing, sleeping, playing, and keeping watch, and Costco is currently selling a massive 82 inch tall cat tree that’s sure to keep your cats satisfied.

Instagram account CostcoHotFinds spotted this Yaheetech 82.5″ tall cat tree at Costco for just $73.99. The cat tree has multiple levels, including cozy carpeted cat huts, a cat hammock, multiple scratching poles and scratching ramps, a dangling ball toy and rope toy to play with, and plenty of beds for snoozing in and platforms from which to view the world below. This kind of height is great for skittish cats or cats who are sharing a home with multiple animals (or kids) — it gives them a safe place off of the ground they can go to when they start to feel overwhelmed.
