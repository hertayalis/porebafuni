Why did Gi Hun dye his hair red in Squid Game? All You Need To Know

Publish date: 2024-07-22

Gi Hun is a chauffeur and former assembly member who was a chronic gambler. He lost every he once owned to gambling and become highly indebted. He was offered a chance to win loads of cash to pay of his debt. As a desperate man, he jumped to the opportunity not knowing how life threatening it was.

Who is Gi Hun?

Player 456 as he is popularly known goes by the name Gi Hun in the Squid Game. He acted the part of a chronic gambler who lost everything he has worked for. He even lost his wife and child in the same vein. A game was organized for highly indebted persons to participate in and win a huge sum of money that could pay off their debts and set them straight back into life.

As usual, a member is sent to meet and play a simple game with these indebted people who are offered money for slaps should they lose the game. After every round of meeting, a card is giving to the indebted person as a ticket into the Squid Game. Seemed a good deal until they finally started the game when it all came out that only one participant can come out victorious and alive.

What is Gi Hun age, height and weight?

Hun is 47 years old. He is 5 feet 11 inches tall and weighs 78 kg.

What is Gi Hun’s Nationality and Ethnicity?

Gi Hun is a South Korean from Ssangmun-dong, Seoul, South Korea.

What is Gi Hun’s profession?

Gi Hun was a chauffeur and an ex-assembly worker. He became so indebted because of his excessive love for gambling. He offer to join to play a series of childhood games so to win the ash prize to settle his debts.

Why did he get red hair in Squid Game?

Gi Hun who was contestant number 456 got his hair dyed red to portray the intrinsic anger he has for the organizes of the Squid Game. It shows also that it is by far the craziest thing one can do to his hair in any situation. Red symbolizes anger and rage in most contexts so that was what he wanted to achieve dying his hair red.

Why did 456 dye his hair red?

He dyed his hair red in an attempt to vent his anger, rage and frustration towards the organizes of the game.

Who is Gi Hun married to?

He was married to Kang Eun-ji. They got separated after his excessive gambling got him bankrupt and he could no longer fend for his family.

Does Gi Hun have children?

Yes, Gi Hun has a daughter with his former wife, Kang Eun-ji. After Gi Hun went broke, his wife left him taking away his only child too. Most of the dispute between Gi Hun and his wife was entered around their daughter.
