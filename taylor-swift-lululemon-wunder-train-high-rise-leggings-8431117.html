Taylor Swift Wore the Comfy Lululemon Leggings I Rely on Like Clockwork

Publish date: 2024-06-29

When I say “leggings,” more strenuous activities like running, the gym, and weight-lifting might come to mind, but if you’re like me, comfy Sundays in, movie nights, and lounging on the couch may take center stage. However you attribute the stretchy pants, there’s no doubt they’re one of the most superior bottoms you could own — and Taylor Swift just slipped on her own pair. 

The singer, who’s been giving us look after look, went casual in an oversized Adidas fleece jacket — which has since sold out due to the Swift effect. Its bold animal print made a statement, while the subdued colors kept it low key. Adidas x Ivy Park sneakers completed her cool-girl look, though they’re no longer available, either. You get the picture — when Swift wears something, it sells out. So, I was more than shocked when I found out that her exact Lululemon Wunder Train leggings were still in stock.

Lululemon Wunder Train High-Rise Leggings

The high-rise leggings are available in seven colors, including Swift’s classic black, a sky blue, and fiery red. The pants are available in sizes 0 to 14, four lengths, and are Lululemon’s fastest-drying tights in its collection, meaning sweat wicks away with ease. Thanks to the bottoms’ breathable material, you can also beat the heat while working out by staying cool.

Swift’s Lululemon leggings additionally include a hidden pocket and drawstring cord within the waistband to ensure that your pants stay up. But it’s the medium support and four-way stretch for me. Comfortably wear these leggings to a cycling class, gym session, or just around the house; they’re sturdy enough for high-intensity workouts but stretchy enough for relaxation.

You may be asking, “Ruby, how do you know all of this is even true?” Well, I’ve tested the pants myself, and I’m here to tell you that they’re beyond worth the $98 price. I’ve worn my own Wunder Train leggings to hot yoga and have stayed fresh all class long. Better yet, I’ve never left the studio with sweat marks on my backside — that’s a win in and of itself. And don’t even get me started on styling these bad boys. I’ve worn my versatile Lululemon leggings with oversized sweatshirts, Ugg boots, crop tops, and beyond. Not to mention, the fabric is thick enough to conceal my panty lines, which deserves an award all on its own.

Shoppers are just as impressed, claiming that the Wunder Train leggings are “so soft, “comfortable,” and even contour to the body. “I wasn’t a fan of Lululemon until I discovered Wunder Train,” said another reviewer. The fabric has “the perfect amount of compression,” they continued. A different fan said they always wore the Lululemon Align leggings, but “like these so much better,” while someone else insisted that the Wunder Trains don’t “feel like you are wearing anything.” Shoppers don’t have to “fight them to stay up,” either, though many do go up a size for that extra perfect fit.  

There’s no denying it; the Lululemon Wunder Train leggings are it. But, if they follow the trend of Swift’s sold-out separates, I doubt they'll stay in stock for long.
