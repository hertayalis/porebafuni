Biography:

Josie Loren was born on March 19, 1987 in Miami, Florida, USA. She studied high school at New World School of the Arts in Miami where she took stage acting lessons. She then attended UCLA, studying Mass Media Communication with a minor in Spanish.

In 2006, she made her television debut with the series Veronica Mars. She has since appeared in other shows including Hannah Montana, Medium, Drake & Josh, Cory in the House, and The Bill Engvall Show.

She went on to make her first major role as Kaylie Cruz in Make It or Break It, which she played from 2009 to 2012. She also starred as Angela Morrissey in the Disney Channel Original Movie Hatching Pete.
