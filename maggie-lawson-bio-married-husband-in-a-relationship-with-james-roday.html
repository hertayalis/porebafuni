Maggie Lawson Bio

Maggie Lawson is from Louisville, Kentucky, USA. She was born on August 12, 1980 into the family of Mike and Judy Lawson. Her father worked as a hotel manager while her mother was a conscientious housewife. She spent her early years in Louisville with her parents and two siblings named Chris and Nick Lawson.

Growing up, Maggie developed a keen interest in show business at the age of eight. While attending St. Stephen Martyr Elementary School, she performed regularly in the local theater performances. She later attended Assumption High School in Louisville, where she also performed in several plays organized by the school. After graduating, she was admitted to the University of Louisville, where she earned her bachelor’s degree. During her studies she was part of the school’s theater group and appeared in various theater productions on a number of occasions.
