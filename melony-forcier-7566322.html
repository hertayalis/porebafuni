Title: Contributing Writer

Education: Boston University

Melony is a full-time freelance writer with over two years of experience in fashion and beauty editorial. She is a graduate of Boston University’s College of Communication, where she received a Bachelor of Science degree in journalism. She previously worked as a content update editor for Dotdash Meredith brands, including Byrdie, Brides, and InStyle. She’s contributed to outlets including Byrdie, Brides, InStyle, Who What Wear, and Allure.
