Demi Moore's Dog Pilaf Sat Front Row at the Versace Fashion Show

Publish date: 2024-05-24

Demi Moore sat front row at the latest Versace fashion show, but her plus one may just have stolen the spotlight from her. No, we're not talking about any of her famous kids, it's another member of the family that's getting fans everywhere to ooh and ahhh: Pilaf, the family's beloved dog. While Moore took in the latest collection from her pal Donatella Versace — models like Gigi Hadid, Kendall Jenner, and Natalia Bryant walked the show — she had Pilaf in her lap (and Valentino designer Pierpaolo Piccioli also brought his dog, Miranda). Moore wore a pair of super-blingy fringed pants to the show and a blingy embellished blazer.

The Versace show isn't the only place Moore has taken Pilaf during this trip. Just yesterday, Moore was spotted with Pilaf out and about in Milan. Moore wore a low-key 'fit, slipping into a black sweatshirt as she carried Pilaf in a sling across her body. The dog looked super chill and happy to be along for the ride. 

Moore's Instagram posts have ensured that her 2-year-old Chihuahua has cemented its status as a social media star. Not only is the dog in Moore's posts from home, but Pilaf has also accompanied her on various trips and to major events. Moore even submitted Pilaf for a very special distinction: Guinness World Records' World's Shortest Living Dog title, according to People. 

Moore hasn't offered up any updates on whether or not Pilaf dethroned Pearl, who had the title as of April 2023, but we're sure that if and when she does nab any sort of commendation, the little pup's proud mom will share the news everywhere.
