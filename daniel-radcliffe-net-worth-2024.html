The Harry Potter Franchise

The foundation of Daniel Radcliffe’s wealth undoubtedly comes from his role as Harry Potter in the eponymous film series. Beyond his acting salary, Radcliffe also benefited from the merchandising and royalties associated with the Harry Potter brand. This includes revenue from DVD sales, merchandise, video games, and more.
