Leo Resig net worth: Leo Resig is an American internet CEO and entrepreneur who has a net worth of $50 million. Leo Resig is the co-owner of the incredibly popular website theCHIVE.com.

Leo founded the company Resignation Media with his brother John Resig in August 2007. Resignation Media is also the parent company of theBERRY.com, theBRIGADE.com, TheChivery.com and theTHROTTLE.com. Leo attended Indiana University, John attended Hanover College. After John graduated, he moved to Los Angeles to become an actor, Leo eventually followed his brother out west. Together they launched theCHIVE in November 2008. Today theCHIVE receives over 20 million visits per month and generates over 100 million pageviews. According to a Bloomberg Businessweek article, the company also earns over $100 million in revenue per year. The Resigs grew up in Fort Wayne, Indiana. The recently moved the entire company from Los Angeles to Austin, Texas in order to escape California's extraordinarily high tax rates.
