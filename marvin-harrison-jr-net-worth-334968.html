About

Star football player and athlete who gained fandom as a wide receive for the Ohio State University's Buckeyes. In late 2023, he was widely considered one of the top talents going into the 2024 National Football League Draft.

He played high school football at St. Joseph's Preparatory School. He helped the school win three state championships during his time there.

He received offers to play college football at the University of Florida and University of Notre Dame. At the end of the 2022 season he was named All-American. He has more than 300,000 followers on his verified Instagram account.

His father is NFL Hall of Famer Marvin Harrison.
