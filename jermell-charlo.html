All you need to know about Jermell Charlo

Publish date: 2024-05-19

Jermell Charlo is an American professional boxer, with a net worth of $2 million. Jermell Charlo, not to be confused with his identical twin brother, is most known for his upcoming fight against Saul “Canelo” Alvarez on September 30, 2023.

Who is Jermell Charlo?

Growing up in Richmond, Texas, Jermell Charlo and his twin brother, Jermall Charlo, were exposed to the sport of boxing from a young age. Their intrinsic skill was obvious even then. This environment fostered their common passion, ultimately leading them down parallel paths to boxing prominence. The Charlo twins, as they are generally known, trained relentlessly, pushing each other to new heights, resulting in a familial and professional closeness.

How old is Jermell Charlo?

He is currently 33 years old.

What is Jermell Charlo’s net worth?

He is estimated to be worth $2 Million.

What is Jermell Charlo’s career?

Jermell Charlo’s amateur career was nothing short of outstanding. He competed in over 100 battles, winning an incredible amount of fights and demonstrating his potential as a future professional boxing great. Even in those early days, he was clearly committed to the sport. Each match, each triumph, sharpened his abilities and prepared him for the challenges that were ahead in the professional world.

Charlo made his professional boxing debut in 2007 and rapidly established himself in the light middleweight division. His style, paired with his agility and force, captivated both fans and critics. Throughout his professional career, Charlo has fought a wide range of opponents, consistently demonstrating his mettle.

His record is littered with victories, several of which came via knockout, cementing his reputation as a formidable opponent in the ring. With each bout, Charlo secured his place in the boxing world, earning the respect and adoration of peers and fans.

Several matches in Charlo’s remarkable career stand out as particularly significant. One of the most memorable came against Gabriel Rosado in 2014. This match was a tribute to Charlo’s boxing abilities, as he won by unanimous decision, raising his profile in the boxing community.

Charlo’s next important fight was against John Jackson in 2016, where he demonstrated his resilience and determination. After encountering hurdles early in the bout, he came back with a knockout, showcasing his never-say-die spirit.

One of his most well-known fights was against Tony Harrison in 2018. In an unexpected turn of events, Charlo suffered his first professional defeat. However, true to his spirit, he came back stronger in their 2019 rematch and reclaimed the WBC.
