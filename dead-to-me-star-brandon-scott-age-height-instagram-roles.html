Dead to Me star Brandon Scott age, height, Instagram, roles

Publish date: 2024-07-11

Brandon Scott is an American actor and producer known for portraying Detective Nick Prager in the Netflix dark comedy series Dead to Me. The youngest of three, Brandon studied at New York University and received his BFA from the Tisch School of the Arts. He married actress Jenn Liu in 2017.

Outside of Dead to Me, you might recognize Brandon from his roles in shows like Grey’s Anatomy, This Is Us, and Channel Zero. He also voiced Henry in the video game The Last of Us.

For those curious to learn more about the actor, keep reading to find out where you can follow him on social media and more!

Brandon is 40 years old. He was born on July 30, 1982, in Tuscaloosa, Alabama, making his star sign Leo. He is 6 feet tall with black hair and light brown eyes.

You can follow the actor at @brandonoscott. Brandon isn’t super active on his social media account, usually posting about once or twice a month to promote his latest project. His most recent posts are promos for Dead to Me season 3. He has a relatively conservative following with under 10k followers, but we’re sure that will grow once people watch the final season of Dead to Me.

Brandon is a talented actor who has been working steadily since 2005. One of his earliest roles was a recurring spot on Grey’s Anatomy, where he played Dr. Ryan Spalding. He has also voiced characters in video games like The Last of Us and Uncharted 4: A Thief’s End.

In 2019, Brandon joined the cast of the NBC series This Is Us as Cory Lawrence, and he was previously in the cast of Netflix’s teen drama series 13 Reasons Why as Coach J.J. Kerba.
