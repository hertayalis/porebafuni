Where Is Luke List From?

Luke List was born and raised in Seattle, Washington, but now lives in Augusta, Georgia. He was raised by his parents Bonny List and father Mark List.

He attended Baylor School in Chattanooga, Tennessee, for high school. In 2007, he graduated from Vanderbilt University and began his professional career.

List won his first professional match on the Nationwide Tour on April 29, 2012, at the South Georgia Classic.
