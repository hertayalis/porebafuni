Stranger Things Season 4 Actor Logan Riley Bruner Wiki | Bio

25-years-old Logan Riley Bruner (born on July 26, 1997) is a Newyorker actor who has been in the acting industry since 2002. He started his career with short movies and later moved on to some more significant projects. You might have witnessed his performance in a 2020 show named “The Plot Against America,” where his role was of a newsboy.

In 2022, this young American actor climbed the ladder of success after landing as Fred Benson in the 4th season of Netflix’s “Stranger Things,” released on July 1, 2020. Besides being a fantastic actor, Logan is also an unmatched writer. His writing skills are superb, and our sources revealed that he is currently involved in making a horror film, where he is working as an actor and as a co-writer.

Career

During the early years of his career, Riley mostly worked in short movies, some of which he directed. There was also a time when he had nothing to do but instead of wasting that time, he used it and learned martial arts.

There are more than 12 short-movie in which Logan worked, including “Ex Oblivione,” “Dream Lover,” “Bombshell,” “Best Friends,” “Giselle’s Heart,” “Ebenezer the Traveler,” “Vox Lux,” and “Exploits of Daddy B.” He also visited Canada for the shoot of an Apple commercial.

He also worked on a few shows like “Mozart in the Jungle,” “Friends of the People,” and “Orange Is the New Black,” but in all of those, he either played a side role or an uncredited role.

Luckily in 2019, he landed the role of Lance in “The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel,” and his career took off. In 2020, he was part of different productions like “Little America,” “Tucci & Jones,” “For Life,” and in 2021, “FBI.”

The two bachelor’s degree holder Riley is a very talented artist, and his career is booming. There is no doubt he would one day become a celebrity.
